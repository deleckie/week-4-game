How to Play The Crysal Game

  • You will be give a random number at the beging of the game.
  • There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a spasific point value to your total score.
  • You win the game by maching you total score to the random number genorated at the start of the game.
  • You lose the game if your score exceeds the randomly genorated number.
  • Good luck and enjoy!
